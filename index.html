Brand

Being engineering and computer science students ourselves we were all familiar with the notion of a weekend spent on our computers, laughing and joking with our peers, while simultaneously doing what we all love. Building...and creating. But this one would be different, it would be a celebration of diversity, a jamboree of inclusiveness

an entire weekend dedicated to fostering an environment in which minorities would feel empowered to harness their creative, technical, and innovative skills to produce something meaningful...and thus, Equals Equals was born.

About Us

A

Equals Equals is a new kind of hackathon meant to introduce and expose underrepresented minorities to careers in technology and technology entreprenuirship. It's kind of like a design sprint and a hackathon had a baby, and it's name was Equals Equals. Communityy and inclusion breeds innovation , excitement, and motivation to solve some pretty tough problems. Our hope is that this event will not only get underrepresented groups excited about technology and it's capabilities, but also about the impact it can have on the issues and communities that they are most passionate about.
